## Manuscript Guidelines

Accepted file types: `.docx` or `.pdf`.

- Use 12pt Times New Roman
- Double-spaced text
- 1 inch margins all sides
- Title page with book title and author name

## Upload Steps

1. Login to your dashboard
2. Go to **Manuscripts**
3. Click **Upload Manuscript**
4. Select and submit your file

Need templates? Check **Resources** in your dashboard.
