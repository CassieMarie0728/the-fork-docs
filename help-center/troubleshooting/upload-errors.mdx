---
title: Upload Errors
description: Fix problems uploading manuscripts or covers.
---

## Symptoms

- Error: "File type not supported"
- Upload stalls or fails

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check file format">
    Only `.docx` or `.pdf` for manuscripts,
    `.jpg` or `.png` for cover images.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File size under 10MB">
    Large files will fail. Compress or split your document if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    Sometimes browser plugins cause issues. Use Chrome or Firefox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
