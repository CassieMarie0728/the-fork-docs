## Add Your Bio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    In your dashboard, click **Profile** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Author Bio">
    Click **Edit** next to the Author Bio section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write or Paste Bio">
    Briefly introduce yourself, your genre, and interests. Max 500 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save**. Your bio now appears on your public author page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
